## Data Availability

The raw farmland cadastral records for Bishan District and Neijiang Dongxing District derive from China's Third National Land Survey and cannot be publicly redistributed under existing data-governance restrictions. The manuscript provides open reproduction tracks for the synthetic farmland benchmark, the public-data Buchanan County boundary check, and the training/planning diagnostics. Derived data products support verification and re-analysis of the real-county results without redistributing raw parcel geometries, as described in the manuscript's Data Availability section.
