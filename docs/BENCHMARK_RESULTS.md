# Synthetic Benchmark Results — Full 175-cell Sweep

5 methods (Random, Greedy, GA, PPO, MPC) × 7 presets × 5 seeds = **175 runs**.
Generated by `scripts/aggregate_results.py`. All values are mean ± sample standard deviation (n=5).

## Method shorthand

- **Random**: uniform pair sampling under the same exchange budget
- **Greedy**: connectivity-aware local search, single-step lookahead
- **GA**: NSGA-II population search (50-pair budget on synth, 500 on real)
- **PPO**: model-free DRL with parcel-scoring policy
- **MPC**: contrastive world model + horizon-5 random shooting (this paper)

## Notes on the headline numbers

- `bishan_clone` / `neijiang_clone` are calibrated synthetic clones of the two real counties used in the paper, not new measurements; treat them as reproducibility anchors.
- MPC was trained with `lambda_rank=5`, 5-model ensemble, horizon=5, top_k=50, 1000 states, 50 actions per step (CPU only).
- PPO was trained with 100k timesteps per cell. Several plain_large/plain_medium_frag cells hit the 20h Colab session cap and were resumed from checkpoint.
- Random/Greedy/GA results came from the Plan A generator pass on commit `94e3742`; PPO/MPC came from the Plan B sweep on `09f7855`.
